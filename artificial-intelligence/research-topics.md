# Suggested Research Topics

Open questions and exploration ideas that go beyond the curriculum. This is a dump zone, like the resources page: any interesting research direction gets added quickly, and periodic passes will organize entries and cross-reference them to specific parts, chapters, and topics.

Each entry should eventually carry: a short description, why it matters, prerequisites from the curriculum, and starting references.

## Ideas

1. Fitting a surrogate LLM to the inputs and outputs of a target LLM as an analysis method: where the surrogate matches and where it diverges maps which parts of the target's behavior are simple versus deep. Connects to knowledge distillation, model extraction, and surrogate-based explainability.
2. Expressing user requirements to an LLM as formal mathematical specifications (pre/postconditions, invariants, temporal logic) and verifying the generated software mathematically against them, replacing plausible code with proven code. Connects to formal methods (Dafny, Lean, TLA+), program synthesis, and generate-verify-repair loops; the open frontier is translating informal intent into formal specs.
